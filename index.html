Essentials before landing/after landing. [1/4]

  • [X] Valid Passport (at least 6 months before expiration)
  • [ ] Apply for K-ETA (Visa Exemption). Cost: 10,000 KRW ($7-8). Needs to be done 72 hours before entry.
  • [ ] Purchase eSIM for Internet Connection. Recommended Carriers: Korea Telecom, Ubigi. Cost: $10-25 depending on the plan. Can be purchased at the airport as well!
  • [ ] Install necessary apps:
    • [ ] GPS: KakaoMap / Naver Map
    • [ ] Translation: Papago
  • [ ] Money. Revolut seems to work on most global ATMs (mainly banks and not 7-11). Send Travel Notice to Revolut.
    • [ ] Issue New Revolut Physical Card.
  • [ ] Maybe Discover Seoul Pass / Tmoney Card (Used to visit attractions pay for public transportation) Detailed Info + Support for Iphone late 2025.

Airport to Seoul

  1. #Recommended# Fastest method is the Express Arex Train to Seoul Station. Cost is around $8 (Free with Discover Seoul Pass)
  2. Bus for specific hotels or not in the center of Seoul.
  3. Taxi.
  4. Car Rental (not recommended).

Accomodation

  • Seoul
    1. Center of Seoul. 5 min walk from Seoul Station. 2 Bedroom. 100€ each for 2 nights.
  • Hongdae
    1. Hanok Mood. 5 min walk from Hongdae Station. 2 Bedroom. 119€ each for 2 nights.
  • Sinchon

    Western Seoul. More traditional Korean vibe. Mellow vibe. Yonsei University.

    1. H Avenue Hotel. 360€ per couple for 2 nights.
    2. Casa Amarilla. 6 minutes on foot from Exit 6 of Hongik University Station. 84€ each for 2 nights.
