Accomodation

  • Seoul

    Best location. Close to everything. Seoul Station easy access by rail to Busan (KTX trains). Quite slummy, protests, even cult stands.

    1. Center of Seoul. 5 min walk from Seoul Station. 2 Bedroom. 100€ each for 2 nights.
  • Hongdae

    Northern Western Seoul. For young travelers. Different atmosphere day/night. Day: trending restaurants, café, clothing shops. Wild Night life ==> Maybe too noisy.

    1. Duplex Hongik.
    2. Hanok Mood. 5 min walk from Hongdae Station. 2 Bedroom. 119€ each for 2 nights.
  • Sinchon

    Western Seoul. More traditional Korean vibe. Mellow vibe. Yonsei University.

    1. H Avenue Hotel. 360€ per couple for 2 nights.
    2. Casa Amarilla. 6 minutes on foot from Exit 6 of Hongik University Station. 84€ each for 2 nights.
