*** TODO Essentials before landing/after landing. [1/6]

- [X] Valid Passport (at least 6 months before expiration)
- [ ] =Optional= Apply for [[https://www.k-eta.go.kr/portal/newapply/index.do][K-ETA]](Visa Exemption). Not Needed till 2027 but allows for benefits like skipping the arrival card. Cost: 10,000 KRW (6€). Needs to be done at least 72 hours before entry.
- [ ] Purchase eSIM for Internet Connection. Recommended Carriers: [[https://roaming.kt.com/esim/eng/main.asp][Korea Telecom]], [[https://cellulardata.ubigi.com/data-plans-and-coverage/ubigi-esim-data-plans/?destination=kor&one-off=on&monthly=on&annual=on#][Ubigi]]. Cost: 10-25€ depending on the plan. *Can be purchased at the airport as well!*
- [ ] Install necessary apps:
  - [ ] GPS: KakaoMap / Naver Map
  - [ ] Translation: Papago
  - [ ] Chatting: Kakao Talk (used mainly for reservations)
  - [ ] Taxis: Kakao Taxi
- [ ] Money. Revolut seems to work on most global ATMs (mainly banks and not 7-11). Send Travel Notice to Revolut.
  - [ ] Issue New Revolut Physical Card.
- [ ] Issue WowPass for a cash free experience. One card for evertyhing. [[https://www.reddit.com/r/koreatravel/comments/1oaj5b5/comparing_transit_cards_pros_and_cons_tmoney_ezl/][Detailed Info on all cards + Support for Iphone late 2025.]]
